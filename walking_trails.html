Eventually there will also be a greenway in this area, (the extenstion of the Blessington greenway coming next year), details such as car parks and entrances will be listed below.




Walking Trials


Radio Tower Trail

This short and sweet forset trail is an out and back trail through a forested area up to a local radio tower with a stunning view of the surrounding area. It is dog and child friendly and is available on the All Trials App. There is a car park at the base of the trail, and the trail is mostly used by locals for runnng, hiking and dog walking. There are no facilities located at the trail a part from the car park so make sure you follow Ireland's "Leave No Trace" policy.
